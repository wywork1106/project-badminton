Who We Are

USRSA

United States Racquet Stringers Association - The global leader in racquet stringing education and certification.

CRSF

China Racquet Stringing Federation - Pioneering professional stringing services in China with experienced stringers.

MSRSF

Malaysia Racquet Stringing Federation - People who are passionate about this sport and racket. To provide and share the vision to all people.

MSRSF Vision

To be the best and most professional stringing service provider in Malaysia, setting the gold standard for racket stringing excellence and education.

Our Journey

First International Course

March 3-7, 2025

CRSF organized a team of experienced domestic stringers to travel to Malaysia to conduct CS International Certification Professional Stringer Training. This was the first time that a Chinese local stringer organization achieved cross-border technical export, marking the international recognition of our country's technical strength in the field of sports equipment services.

MSRSF Establishment

2025

Founded as Malaysia's first dedicated racket stringing training center, bringing international expertise to local stringers and enthusiasts.
